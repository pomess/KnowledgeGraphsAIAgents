# Universitat Pompeu Fabra

Public research university in Barcelona, Spain. Known for strong programs in economics, political science, and engineering.

## Relevance

[[bruno-manzano|Bruno Manzano]] earned his degree in Superior Engineering in Computer Science (taught in English) from UPF, spanning 2021–2025. This program provided the technical foundation for his career in AI/ML engineering.
