# Disease360

Active project engagement between [[deloitte|Deloitte]] and [[almirall|Almirall]]. Disease360 is a medical deep research initiative. The MVP product within this project is [[vera|Vera]], an AI-powered medical research assistant.

## Product

[[vera|Vera]] — a medical deep research assistant built with LangChain's DeepAgents framework. Vera has access to:

- **PubMed** — biomedical literature
- **KOLs** — key opinion leaders
- **EMA** — European Medicines Agency regulatory data
- **FDA** — US Food and Drug Administration regulatory data
- **GlobalData** — pharmaceutical and healthcare intelligence
- **Clinical Trials** — clinical trial registries
