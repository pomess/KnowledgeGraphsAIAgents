# Bruno Manzano

AI Engineer based in Barcelona, Spain. Owner and curator of this wiki.

## Profile

Bruno Manzano is an AI Engineer currently working at [[deloitte|Deloitte]]'s T&T Artificial Intelligence team in Barcelona. He holds a degree in Computer Science from [[upf-barcelona|Universitat Pompeu Fabra]] (2021–2025) and speaks Spanish (native), Catalan (native), and English (proficient).

His professional focus is on building autonomous AI agents and transitioning proof-of-concepts into production-ready [[agentic-workflows|agentic workflows]]. His career has progressed through four roles in Barcelona's AI ecosystem — from intern to ML engineer to AI engineer — all centered on applied AI.
