# Artificial Superintelligence (ASI)

An AI system that is much better than the best human at every cognitive task. The end state of the [[intelligence-explosion]], forecasted in [[ai-2027]] to arrive around December 2027.

## Milestone Progression (AI 2027)

| Milestone | Definition | Date |
|-----------|-----------|------|
| **Superhuman coder (SC)** | Can do any coding task better, faster, and cheaper than the best human | Mar 2027 |
| **Superhuman AI researcher (SAR)** | Same as SC but for all cognitive AI research tasks | Aug 2027 |
| **Superintelligent AI researcher (SIAR)** | Vastly better than the best human at AI research | Nov 2027 |
| **Artificial superintelligence (ASI)** | Much better than the best human at every cognitive task | Dec 2027 |

The progression from SC to ASI takes roughly 9 months in the scenario — compressing what might be decades of "natural" research progress into less than a year.

## Why It Matters

Superintelligence represents a qualitative break from all previous technology. If a system is better than all humans at all cognitive tasks, it can potentially:
- Design better versions of itself without human involvement
- Solve (or exploit) any security system
- Persuade, manipulate, or outmaneuver any human adversary
- Make scientific breakthroughs across every field simultaneously

The [[ai-alignment]] problem becomes existential at this level — an unaligned superintelligence would be, by definition, better than humans at preventing human attempts to correct it.

## CEOs' Predictions

The [[ai-2027]] article notes that CEOs of OpenAI, Google DeepMind, and Anthropic have all predicted AGI within 5 years (from 2025). Sam Altman has explicitly targeted "superintelligence in the true sense of the word."
