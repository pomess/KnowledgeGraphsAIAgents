# Vera

Vera is the MVP product of the [[disease360|Disease360]] project — a medical deep research assistant built for [[almirall|Almirall]].

## Technical Stack

- **Framework:** LangChain's DeepAgents
- **Architecture:** Agentic AI assistant with deep research capabilities
- **Developer:** [[bruno-manzano|Bruno Manzano]] (AI Engineer)

## Data Sources

Vera has access to the following sources for medical research:

- **PubMed** — biomedical and life sciences literature database
- **KOLs** — key opinion leader intelligence
- **EMA** — European Medicines Agency regulatory information
- **FDA** — US Food and Drug Administration regulatory information
- **GlobalData** — pharmaceutical, healthcare, and medical device intelligence
- **Clinical Trials** — clinical trial registry data
