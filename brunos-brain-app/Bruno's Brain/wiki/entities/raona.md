# Raona

Barcelona-based technology consultancy. [[bruno-manzano|Bruno Manzano]]'s first employer, where he worked as AI Engineer Intern (Oct 2023–Jun 2024) and then AI Engineer (Jun–Aug 2024).

## Relevance

Bruno's time at Raona was foundational — his first professional AI/ML experience. Key accomplishments:

- Engineered a **document classifier for Cellnex using BERT** that categorized over 3 million documents. This was a large-scale NLP deployment.
- Designed NLP systems that improved client engagement.
- Maintained AI infrastructure for high availability across applications.
- Developed and deployed ML models tailored to client business challenges.

The BERT document classifier is notable as Bruno's first major production ML system at scale.
