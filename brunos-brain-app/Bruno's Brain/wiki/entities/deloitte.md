# Deloitte

Global professional services firm. [[bruno-manzano|Bruno Manzano]] works as an AI Engineer in the T&T (Technology & Transformation) Artificial Intelligence team at the Barcelona office (Sep 2025–present).

## Relevance

Bruno's role at Deloitte focuses on building autonomous AI agents and transitioning proof-of-concepts to production-ready [[agentic-workflows|agentic workflows]]. He partners with clients to translate business requirements into technical reality through continuous feedback loops and iterative refinement.

This is Bruno's current position and his first role at a global firm, following positions at smaller Barcelona-based companies ([[raona|Raona]], [[ctti|CTTI]]).
