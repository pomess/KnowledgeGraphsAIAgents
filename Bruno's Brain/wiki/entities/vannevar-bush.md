# Vannevar Bush

American engineer, inventor, and science administrator (1890–1974). Director of the Office of Scientific Research and Development during World War II, coordinating wartime scientific research including the Manhattan Project.

## Relevance to This Wiki

Bush is cited in the [[llm-wiki-idea-file]] as the intellectual ancestor of the LLM Wiki pattern, through his 1945 essay "As We May Think" in which he proposed the [[memex]].

## Key Contribution: The Memex

In "As We May Think" (The Atlantic, July 1945), Bush described the [[memex]] — a hypothetical personal device for storing and navigating a vast library of documents connected by associative trails. His vision anticipated hypertext, personal knowledge management, and the LLM Wiki pattern itself.

> Bush's vision was closer to [the LLM Wiki] than to what the web became: private, actively curated, with the connections between documents as valuable as the documents themselves. The part he couldn't solve was who does the maintenance. — [[llm-wiki-idea-file]]
